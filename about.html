how I work

I approach quality engineering as the full journey — from planning to release. I create clear test strategies, write detailed test cases, and execute manual testing to ensure coverage where automation doesn’t fit. Alongside that, I design maintainable automation frameworks using Playwright, WebdriverIO, and Cypress to give fast and reliable feedback in CI/CD. I validate APIs with Postman and Rest Assured, dig into data with SQL, and make sure defects are reported clearly with actionable detail. My goal is always to provide the right balance of manual and automated testing so that teams release with confidence and users trust the product.
